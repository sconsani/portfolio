Hi, I'm a front-end web developer that loves colorful and interactive designs.

A little bit about me.

I am from the Bay Area, California and graduated from UCLA with a degree in Fine Arts. My previous focus in art and design led me to a 5 year career in graphic design, product photography and website content management. As I transitioned into managing the content and front end of two retail websites I discovered my interests in problem solving, content management and user experience.

I find the effects of technology on coming generations a crucial reminder to stay aware in what we choose to build today and how and why we choose to build it- keeping in mind the mental health and wellness of humanity. When I'm not creating, I love to travel, go camping, skateboarding, juggling, and reading books about psychology and neuroscience.
