Hi, I'm a front-end web developer that loves colorful and interactive designs.

A little bit about me.

I am from the Bay Area, California and graduated from UCLA with a degree in Fine Arts. My previous focus in art and design led me to a 5 year career in graphic design, product photography and website content management.

As I transitioned into managing the two retail websites I discovered my interests in problem solving, user experience and translating my knowledge of design into beautifully coded websites.

I am currently a project manager and front end web developer at a health and wellness startup. I'm looking to join an engineering team as a front-end web developer to expand my knowledge and contribute to a fun and supportive team!

When I'm not creating, I love to travel, go camping, skateboarding, juggling, and reading books about psychology and neuroscience.

Confidence in Problem Solving

My passion behind my work is in getting to solve problems and constantly create better solutions. I am confident in researching, reading documentation and analyzing other code or projects to design a solution.

Organization & Communication
